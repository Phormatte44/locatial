## Important distinction

The corpus has several connected layers, but they should not be merged into one giant document.

- Brand defines why the product exists.
- Editorial defines what Locatial notices and how it describes it.
- Data defines how observations are represented.
- The graph defines how places and concepts relate.
- Spatial intelligence projects graph relationships back onto geography.
- The prototype tests whether the whole system creates visible value.
