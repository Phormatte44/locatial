# Open Questions

- Is "The spatial media platform" the permanent category language?
- Which line should lead the public launch: "Make the world readable" or "Make place your medium"?
- What is the final role of "Back into the world"?

## NYC guide

- Which exact neighborhoods belong in the first prototype?
- Is the initial guide a ranked list, a layer-led explorer or both?
- How prominently should confidence appear to end users?
- Should users be able to combine two layers in the first release?

## Data

- Which public and licensed providers are acceptable for the production registry?
- What minimum independent evidence is required before a place contributes to an area field?
- What decay periods apply to prices, hours, crowd character and accessibility?
- Which claims may be venue-supplied but not venue-controlled?

## Spatial intelligence

- Should fields be generated through kernel density, hex grids, street-network weighting or a hybrid?
- How should confidence alter the visual form?
- When should a corridor replace a field?
- What minimum venue density is required before labeling an area?

## Prototype

- Is the prototype intended for user testing, investor communication or engineering validation first?
- Should the initial data fixture use real bars or clearly marked representative data?
- Is the first target mobile web only, or mobile web plus Capacitor shell?
