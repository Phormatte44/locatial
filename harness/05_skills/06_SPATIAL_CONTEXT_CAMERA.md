# Skill — Spatial Context & Camera

Use this for place assignment, map preview, and reader camera framing.

## Required states

- Unassigned
- Candidate
- Assigned

## Camera fields

- Zoom
- Pitch
- Bearing

## Goal

The creator can decide where a Stop is and how the reader should visually arrive there.

## Do not

- Make camera controls mandatory.
- Expose complex camera math early.
- Let the map dominate the writing surface.
