# Locatial Creator Studio — Product Thesis

Locatial Creator Studio is a place-first authoring environment.

It helps creators turn scattered knowledge about places into coherent spatial stories, guides, and collections.

## Core loop

Capture or begin anywhere → define the work → compose meaning → arrange relationships → use the map when helpful → preview → publish → maintain reusable place knowledge.

## Product promise

A creator can collect material, structure a spatial project, write meaning around places, preview the audience journey, publish beautifully, and maintain place knowledge over time.

## Publishing identity

A Profile may own multiple Channels. A Channel is the publishing identity under which Projects are released.

## Not

- Generic page builder
- GIS tool
- Google My Maps clone
- Social network
- Map-first CMS
