# Decision Log

## 2026-06-15 — Library / Projects / Published model

**Status:** Adopted  
**Decision:** Use Library = what you have, Projects = what you make, Published = what people see.

## 2026-06-15 — Dataset lives in Library

**Status:** Adopted  
**Decision:** Datasets are uploaded and managed at Library level, then referenced by Projects.

## 2026-06-15 — Profile / Channel / Project publishing model

**Status:** Adopted  
**Decision:** A Profile may own multiple Channels, and each Channel may publish multiple Projects.

## 2026-06-15 — Camera framing belongs to located Stops

**Status:** Adopted  
**Decision:** Located Stops may include zoom, pitch, and bearing.

## 2026-06-15 — Agent drafts are internal seeding only

**Status:** Adopted  
**Decision:** Agent-generated drafts are internal/admin tooling for Locatial content seeding, not a creator-facing product feature.

## 2026-06-15 — Prototype UI stack

**Status:** Adopted  
**Decision:** First prototype uses React, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, and Obra shadcn_ui kit Pro edition 1.13.2 in neutral dark mode.
